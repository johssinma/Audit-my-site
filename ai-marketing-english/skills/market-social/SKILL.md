## Phase 2: Content Strategy Framework

### 2.1 Content Pillars

Define 4-5 content pillars:

| Pillar # | Type                        | Goal                    | Content Mix                                 |
| -------- | --------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Pillar 1 | **Educational**       | Build authority         | How-to, tips, frameworks, mistakes to avoid |
| Pillar 2 | **Behind the Scenes** | Build trust, humanise   | Process, team, culture                      |
| Pillar 3 | **Social Proof**      | Credibility, conversion | Testimonials, case studies, results         |
| Pillar 4 | **Engagement**        | Community, algorithm    | Questions, polls, debates                   |
| Pillar 5 | **Promotional**       | Revenue, announcements  | Product launches, offers, CTAs              |

**Content Mix:** 40% educational, 20% behind the scenes, 15% social proof, 15% engagement, 10% promotional

## Phase 3: Hook Formulas

### 3.1 Platform-Specific Hooks

**LinkedIn Hooks:**

```
"I [did/learned/lost/gained] [specific thing] and here's what happened:"
"Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take on the industry]"
"[Number] years in [industry]. Here's what nobody tells you:"
"Stop [common practice]. Start [better alternative]. Here's why:"
"I analysed [X] [things] and found [surprising pattern]:"
```

**Twitter/X Hooks:**

```
"Here's a thread on [topic] nobody is talking about:"
"[Contrarian statement]. Let me explain."
"[Number] things I wish I'd known about [topic] [time] ago:"
"The difference between [good thing] and [great thing]:"
"Hot take: [bold claim]"
```

**Instagram Hooks (Captions & Reels):**

```
"Save this for later" (educational carousel)
"I tested [X] for [time]. Results inside."
"[Number] signs you [problem] (and how to fix it)"
"My exact [framework/process/system] for [result]:"
```

**TikTok Hooks (First 3 Seconds):**

```
"Wait — are you still doing [old method]?"
"Here's the [topic] hack nobody showed you"
"If you're [audience], watch this"
"The #1 reason your [thing] isn't working"
```

## Phase 4: Hashtag Strategy

Use a tiered approach for every post:

| Tier              | Tag Reach        | Count | Goal                                    |
| ----------------- | ---------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------- |
| **Niche**   | Under 100K posts | 3-5   | Highly targeted, easier to rank         |
| **Medium**  | 100K-1M posts    | 3-5   | Moderate competition, relevant audience |
| **Broad**   | 1M+ posts        | 2-3   | Discovery potential                     |
| **Branded** | Custom           | 1     | Brand recognition, UGC                  |

## Phase 5: Content Repurposing Strategy

### 5.1 The 1-to-10 Framework

Take ONE long-form piece of content and turn it into 10+ social posts:

```
SOURCE: 1 Blog Post / Podcast Episode / YouTube Video / Newsletter

OUTPUTS:
  1. LinkedIn post — Key insight from the piece
  2. Twitter thread — 5-7 key points
  3. Instagram carousel — Main framework visualised
  4. Instagram Reel — 30-second summary of the key point
  5. TikTok — Quick tip format of the #1 takeaway
  6. LinkedIn carousel — PDF slideshow of the framework
  7. Single tweet — The most quotable line
  8. Instagram Story — Behind the scenes of creating it
  9. YouTube Short — Condensed video version
  10. Facebook post — Discussion question on the topic
```
