# Welcome Email Sequence Template (5 Emails)

## Email 1: Welcome & Quick Win (Send: Immediately)

**Subject:** Welcome! Here's your first quick win
**Preview text:** You made the right decision. Let me show you why.

**Body Structure:**

1. Welcome + acknowledge their decision (1-2 sentences)
2. Set expectations — what they'll receive from you (bullet list, 3-4 items)
3. Deliver immediate value — one actionable tip they can use right now
4. Single CTA: Complete your profile / Take the first step

**Key principle:** Deliver value immediately. Don't just say "welcome" — give something useful in the first email.

---

## Email 2: The Problem We Solve (Send: Day 2)

**Subject:** The real reason [problem] keeps happening
**Preview text:** It's not what you think...

**Body Structure:**

1. Describe the problem they're experiencing (empathy)
2. Explain why common solutions don't work (agitate)
3. Present your approach as the solution (resolve)
4. Social proof: A quick result or testimonial
5. CTA: Try [specific feature] or Read [relevant content]

**Key principle:** Show that you understand their pain better than anyone else.

---

## Email 3: Social Proof & Results (Send: Day 4)

**Subject:** How [client] achieved [specific result]
**Preview text:** And you can do the same — here's exactly how.

**Body Structure:**

1. Quick case study: situation → action → result
2. What made the difference (1-2 key insights)
3. How they can get the same result
4. CTA: Start [specific action] now

**Key principle:** Prove it works with real results. Numbers > claims.

---

## Email 4: Overcoming Objection #1 (Send: Day 6)

**Subject:** "I don't have time for [thing]" — Sound familiar?
**Preview text:** Here's the 10-minute version that actually works.

**Body Structure:**

1. Acknowledge the most common objection
2. Reframe it — show why it's easier than they think
3. Give a simplified version / quick-start approach
4. CTA: Try the 10-minute version

**Key principle:** Address the main barrier head-on. Don't ignore it.

---

## Email 5: The Ask (Send: Day 8)

**Subject:** Quick question about your experience
**Preview text:** I'd love your honest feedback.

**Body Structure:**

1. Check in — how is their experience going so far?
2. Ask a specific question (reply-based engagement)
3. Offer help or a resource based on where they are
4. Soft CTA: upgrade to premium, book a call, or explore next steps

**Key principle:** Create a two-way conversation. One reply is worth 10 clicks.

---

## Metrics to Track

* Target open rate: 40-60% (welcome sequences outperform standard campaigns)
* Target click rate: 8-15%
* Target reply rate: 3-5% (especially for Email 5)
* Unsubscribe rate: < 0.5% per email
