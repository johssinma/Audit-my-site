# Content Calendar Template (30 Days)

## Content Pillar Distribution

| Pillar                  | Frequency      | Goal                               |
| ----------------------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Educational (40%)       | 12 posts/month | Build authority, attract audience  |
| Engagement (20%)        | 6 posts/month  | Generate comments, build community |
| Behind the Scenes (15%) | 5 posts/month  | Build trust, show authenticity     |
| Social Proof (15%)      | 5 posts/month  | Build credibility, convert         |
| Promotional (10%)       | 3 posts/month  | Drive sales, increase revenue      |

## Weekly Structure

### Monday — Educational

Start the week with value. Teach something your audience can implement today.

### Tuesday — Engagement

Ask a question, share a strong opinion or run a poll. Get people reacting.

### Wednesday — Educational

Share a framework, analysis or practical guide.

### Thursday — Behind the Scenes / Social Proof

Show the real work, share a win or highlight a client result.

### Friday — Educational / Engagement

End the week with something shareable — tip list, myth-busting or a strong take.

### Weekend — Optional

Lighter content: memes, personal story or reshare of the best-performing post of the week.

## Content Repurposing Workflow

1 long-form piece → 10+ social posts:

1. Blog post or YouTube video (original)
2. Twitter thread with 5-7 key insights
3. LinkedIn article or long-form post
4. Instagram carousel (5-10 slides)
5. TikTok/Reel (60-90s summary)
6. 3-5 quote visuals from key lines
7. Poll or question based on an insight
8. Behind the scenes of the creation process
9. Reshare 2-4 weeks later with a new angle
10. Compile monthly into the newsletter
